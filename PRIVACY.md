# Privacy Policy for Darkument

*Effective Date: June 29, 2026*

Ahmad Hassan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collected from users of the **Darkument** browser extension (the "Extension").

## 1. No Data Collection

Darkument operates **100% locally** in your browser.
* **No Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, or location data).
* **No Browsing History**: We do not track or record the websites you visit, the PDFs you view, or your user activities.
* **No Remote Analytics**: The Extension contains no trackers, telemetry, or analytics scripts.

## 2. Permissions and Usage

The Extension requests the following permissions, which are strictly used to execute its core function locally:
* `activeTab` & `scripting`: Used to temporarily inject the color-inversion overlay on the active document when you click the extension badge.
* `host_permissions` (`<all_urls>` and `file:///*`): Required to enable color inversion across local file paths (such as offline PDFs) and web domains when toggled.
* `notifications`: Used to alert you of configuration options, such as enabling file URL access.
* `tabs`: Used to monitor active tab changes and refresh status in order to update the extension toolbar icon state (ON/OFF).

## 3. Data Transfer & Sharing

Since we do not collect any user data, **we do not sell, trade, or transfer any information** to third parties. No data ever leaves your device.

## 4. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly in this file.
